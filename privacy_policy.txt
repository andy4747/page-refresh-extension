## Privacy Policy

**Page Refresher** is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of information we collect and how we use it.

### Information Collection

- **Personal Information:** The Page Refresher extension does not collect any personal information from users.
- **Usage Data:** The extension does not track or collect any usage data or browsing history.

### Data Usage

- **Functionality:** The extension operates solely to refresh web pages at intervals specified by the user.
- **No Data Sharing:** We do not share or sell any data to third parties.

### Security

- **Data Protection:** As no personal data is collected, there are no security concerns related to user data. However, we implement standard security practices to ensure the extension operates safely.

### Changes to This Policy

- **Updates:** We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted here, and your continued use of the extension constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
